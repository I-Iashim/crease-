Our story

Built on honest tailoring

Since day one

From a single rack to a full wardrobe

Crease Edits started as a small tailoring counter on 5th Road in Rawalpindi — a handful of shirts and one very stubborn belief: clothes should be made to be worn, not just photographed.

Years later, that belief hasn't changed. We've grown into a full western-wear house for men and women — clothing, shoes and accessories — but every piece still has to earn its place on the rack.

Our Mission

To make considered, well-made western clothing accessible — without the markup luxury usually demands.

Our Vision

To be the wardrobe Pakistan reaches for first — recognised for fit, fabric, and finishing, not just trend.

Quality First

Every fabric is tested for wear before it reaches a rack. If it won't hold up after twenty washes, it doesn't ship.

Customer Satisfaction

We fit you, not the other way around

Free size exchanges, a 30-day return window, and a team that actually answers the phone at 0322 9274852 — because a good wardrobe shouldn't come with fine print.
